# Privacy Review

Requires Aarohan review before production publication.

The `/privacy` page contains broad, non-legal privacy principles for the public website and external membership renewal flow. It explains that membership information may be collected through an external Google Form when configured, and that Google may process the interaction.

Before production publication, Aarohan should review:

- exact fields collected in the Google Form
- whether each field is necessary for membership administration
- retention expectations
- who can access form responses
- how members can ask privacy questions
- whether a public privacy contact email should be configured

The current copy explicitly discourages collection of:

- Aadhaar
- passport numbers
- payment card data
- bank account details
- passwords
- unnecessary date of birth
- unnecessary sensitive personal information
